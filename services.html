Services

Support that is individualized, evidence-based, and steady.

I provide compassionate psychiatric care tailored to your goals, pace, and lived experience. My practice integrates medication support, psychotherapy, and wellness-focused support in a way that feels practical and grounded.

Medication Management

Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation and ongoing medication management for depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, trauma, and other mental health concerns.

Psychotherapy

Individual therapy grounded in psychodynamic, cognitive-behavioral, and somatic frameworks. Sessions are 45–60 minutes and typically occur weekly or biweekly.

Integrated Wellness Coaching

Beyond therapy and medication, I support sustainable practices around sleep, nutrition, movement, and stress management to help build long-term wellbeing.

Consultation & Referral

Seeking a second opinion or diagnostic clarity? I offer focused consultations to help you understand your diagnosis, review current treatment, or identify next steps with specialists.
