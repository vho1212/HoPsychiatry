Services

Doing the real work.

Helping you understand yourself with greater clarity and compassion.

Medication Management

Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation and ongoing medication management for depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, ADHD, trauma, substance use, and other mental health concerns. Medication can be a helpful part of treatment for some people, but it's never the only option. If it's appropriate for you, we'll discuss the benefits, risks, and alternatives together so you can make an informed decision that aligns with your goals. If you're already working with a therapist, I'm happy to collaborate with them to coordinate your care.

Psychotherapy

Individual, couples, and family therapy grounded in psychodynamic, cognitive-behavioral, and mindfulness frameworks. I have extensive training in trauma-focused therapy. My work is informed by additional training in psychoanalytic psychotherapy, helping us explore both current challenges and the deeper patterns that shape them. My prior experience in psychedelic-assisted psychotherapy work draws from Internal Family Systems, somatic methods, and compassion-focused therapy. Individual therapy sessions are 45 minutes and are typically held weekly, though some people benefit from meeting twice a week. I also offer couples therapy and family therapy, helping loved ones improve communication and strengthen their relationship.

Integrated Wellness Coaching

Beyond therapy and medication, I support sustainable practices around sleep, nutrition, movement, and stress management to help build long-term wellbeing. My goal is to help you develop a foundation that continues to support your mental health long after our sessions.

Consultation & Referral

Seeking a second opinion or diagnostic clarity? I offer focused consultations to help you understand your diagnosis, review current treatment, or identify next steps with specialists. Every consultation begins with a comprehensive evaluation, with follow-up visits tailored to your individual needs.
