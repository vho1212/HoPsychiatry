FAQ

Common questions about working together.

Here are a few helpful answers about practice logistics, care, and how I approach treatment.

What types of concerns do you work with?

I work with adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, bipolar disorder, and other mood and mental health concerns.

Do you offer medication management and therapy?

Yes. I offer both medication management and psychotherapy, and I can help determine which combination feels most supportive for your needs.

Are you accepting new patients?

At this time, I am not accepting new patient intakes. The waitlist will reopen in November 2027.

Do you work with patients outside California?

I offer telehealth services to patients in California and see patients in person in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

Do you take insurance?

I operate as an out-of-network provider and do not accept insurance directly. Payment is due at the time of service. However, I am happy to provide you with a monthly superbill (an itemized medical receipt) that you can submit to your insurance company for potential out-of-network reimbursement.
