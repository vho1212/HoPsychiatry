02 — Services

Services

I provide compassionate, evidence-based psychiatric care tailored to your individual needs. My practice integrates medication management with psychotherapy and holistic wellness support, drawing on training in psychodynamic, cognitive-behavioral, and somatic approaches. I work collaboratively with you to identify your goals and build a treatment plan that honors your values and lived experience.

Medication Management

Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation and ongoing medication management for depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, trauma, and other conditions. I prioritize finding the right medication at the right dose, with careful monitoring and frequent communication. Sessions typically occur monthly once stabilized.

Psychotherapy

Individual therapy grounded in psychodynamic, cognitive-behavioral, and somatic frameworks. Sessions are 45–60 minutes and typically occur weekly or biweekly. This is space to explore your patterns, process difficult experiences, and develop deeper insight and resilience.

Integrated Wellness Coaching

Beyond medication and therapy, I support you in building sustainable practices around sleep, nutrition, movement, and stress management. Small shifts in daily habits often have profound effects on mood and wellbeing.

Consultation & Referral

Seeking a second opinion or diagnostic clarity? I offer focused consultations to help clarify your diagnosis, review current treatment, or provide referrals to specialists. These can be one-time or brief ongoing consultations as needed.

Insurance & Billing: I am an out-of-network provider. I will provide superbills for you to submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement.
