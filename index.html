A Note From Dr. Ho

Thoughtful, integrative psychiatric care

My approach to psychiatry is grounded in curiosity, collaboration, and deep respect for each person's lived experience. I combine psychotherapy with thoughtful medication management, and I'm deeply interested in the connection between mind and body. I work with adults from all walks of life and have a special commitment to supporting BIPOC and LGBTQ+ communities, women's mental health, trauma, and life transitions.
